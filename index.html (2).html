Kirkpatrick Levels Guide

MEASUREMENT FRAMEWORK

📌 What It Shows

A visual ladder showing the complete training journey across Kirkpatrick levels: SME & stakeholder buy-in (Rung 1) → Knowledge checks (Rung 2) → 30-day performance observations (Rung 3) → Future impact report (Rung 4). Includes job aids, checklists, and eLearning resources at each stage.

💡 Why It Was Designed This Way

This is a visual manifestation of my core differentiator: measuring training at Levels 3 & 4 (behavior change and business results), not just completion and satisfaction. It shows that I design backward from the outcome, not forward from content.

✨ Value It Demonstrates

ROI focus: Every level drives toward business impact. Measurement rigor: Specific tools at each level (observations, checklists, reports). Stakeholder alignment: Shows exactly how learning connects to organizational goals.
