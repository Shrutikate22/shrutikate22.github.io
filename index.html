How I Work

Design Philosophy

I treat training as behavioral intervention, not content delivery. My background in clinical psychology and learning sciences means I design around how people actually learn, believe, and change — using spaced practice, retrieval, cognitive load management, and evidence-based frameworks.

My Approach

I start with the performance gap, not the slide deck. I collaborate with SMEs and stakeholders to diagnose what learners actually need, then build iterative, feedback-driven solutions that I measure through Kirkpatrick Levels 3–4. I'm a self-directed team member who ships on time and communicates clearly.
