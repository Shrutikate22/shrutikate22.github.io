🛠️ How I Work

🧠

Design Philosophy

  • Training = behavioral intervention, not content delivery
  • Design around how people actually learn & change
  • Spaced practice, retrieval, cognitive load management
  • Evidence-based frameworks grounded in learning science
🎯

My Approach

  • Start with the performance gap, not the slide deck
  • Collaborate with SMEs to diagnose real learner needs
  • Build iterative, feedback-driven solutions
  • Measure through Kirkpatrick Levels 3–4
